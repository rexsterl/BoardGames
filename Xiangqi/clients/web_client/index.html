How to Play

  • Red (you) moves first
  • Click a piece to select it
  • Click a highlighted square to move
  • Win by checkmating the opponent's General

Pieces

帥/將 General
Moves one step within palace
仕/士 Advisor
Moves diagonally within palace
相/象 Elephant
Moves 2 steps diagonally, can't cross river
傌/馬 Horse
Moves in L-shape
俥/車 Chariot
Moves any distance orthogonally
炮/砲 Cannon
Jumps one piece to capture
兵/卒 Soldier
Moves forward (and sideways after river)
